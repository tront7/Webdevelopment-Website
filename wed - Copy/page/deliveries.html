We've partnered with a trusted local delivery service to bring our treats to you!

How to Order for Delivery

You can find us on the Mr. D Food and Uber Eats apps. Simply search for "Sweet Delights Bakery," place your order, and let us handle the rest. Enjoy our delicious, handcrafted goods from the comfort of your home or office.

Delivery is available within a 15km radius of our Polokwane store during our regular opening hours.
